Big Data Classification In the common misconception, it is assumed to be categorized by volume only, and this is a misunderstanding since it is categorized according to the definition (3V's) and consists of Size: it is the amount of data derived from a source that defines the importance and capabilities of the data to be classified from the big data. Diversity: means the diversity of data extracted, which helps users, whether researchers or analysts, to choose the appropriate data. Speed: It is the speed of producing and extracting data to cover the demand for it.
